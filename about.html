About Me

Hello everyone! I'm Manoj, a freelance web developer based out in Southall, West London. With over nine years of experience, I specialize in designing, building, and troubleshooting websites for clients. Currently, I am also actively enhancing my skills through Udemy courses.

This website serves as a showcase of my proficiency in HTML, ranging from basic to advanced techniques. While I am well-versed in hand-coding web layouts from scratch using HTML and styling pages with CSS, I decided to undertake this project to further demonstrate my abilities.

My preferred development stack includes WAMP/LAMP (Windows/Linux, Apache, MySQL with PHP). Additionally, I am proficient in utilizing the WordPress CMS and tools like Elementor to create exceptional user experiences and websites. For bespoke web applications, PHP remains my go-to technology for robust back-end development. However, I am open to exploring new technologies as I continue to expand my knowledge.

I am committed to crafting high-quality websites that precisely meet my clients' needs and deliver exceptional user experiences. Whether you have inquiries, wish to discuss a potential project, or simply want to connect and say hi, I would be delighted to hear from you. You can find my contact details on the Contact page. Let's collaborate to bring your vision to life!
